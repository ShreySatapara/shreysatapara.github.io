About Me

Hi,

I am a researcher at Fujitsu Research of India, focusing on generative AI and relevant applications.

I completed my master's degree from the Department of Artificial Intelligence at Indian Institute of Technology Hyderabad (IITH), India, where I was advised by Srijith PK and Maunendra Desarkar. My research focused on Multilingual Natural Language Processing, with my master's thesis on continual learning and cross-lingual transfer in multilingual NLP.

I began my research journey at LDRP Institute of Technology and Research, India, pursuing a Bachelor's in Computer Engineering. My undergraduate research involved detecting hate speech in code-mixed dialogues on social media platforms, supervised by Sandip Modha and Thomas Mandl. Additionally, I worked as a research intern at the Indian Institute of Management Ahmedabad under Hyokjin Kwak, analyzing the impact of Amul Hits by extracting user interactions from social media platforms.
